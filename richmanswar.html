Description

Sit down with your friends for a few quick minutes or a whole group at a party for a nuclear-explosion high-tension filled extravaganza!
Take turns with up to 8 friends building structures, upgrading technology, and taking control of cities around the world in the race to wipe out everyone else before your cities become desolate radioactive wastelands. Controller use is optional. (Controller use has not been tested with the latest version and has potential to malfunction!)
Play either freemode with friends, choosing or randomizing cities, or take on built-in scenarios which offer interesting challenges, real-life recreations, or simply nuke-filled fun!


Instructions

Tutorials on basic game play mechanics and technology upgrades are available in-game.

Left-click menu buttons to select them.

Select arrows to cycle through options for number of players.

Choose Scenarios or Freeplay.

Upgrading range increases how far a city can launch nukes to.

Upgrading production increases how many nukes are produced per turn.

Upgrading economy increases how much money is earned per city per turn.

Building storage facilities will increase the amount of nukes a country can stockpile.

Building response facilities will launch nukes back if shot near.
