Plane Evolution

This game was the submission of team 2004-901 to the Pennsylvania Technology Student Association 2017 Regional Conference.


Description

Control a small Sopwith Camel biplane on a quest to get bigger. Absorb smaller planes to increase in size and technology level to eventually become the gigantic Spruce Goose. Avoid larger and faster planes which will instantly force the player to restart. Read and learn about all of the planes involved in the game.


Instructions

Left-Click Buttons to Select

Plane follows Cursor

Click Play to Begin

Click Exit to Close (Not on HTML)

Click About to Learn

Click Arrows to Cycle Through Info

Hit Escape to Return to Menu
