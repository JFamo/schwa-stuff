Halo


Included here are some maps and gamemodes which I have created for Halo 5. To use them simply sign in to waypoint on the computer, bookmark them, and then use them in-game.

Back

Boogaloo

This gamemode is a horror-style hide and seek. One player is the infected 'boogaloo' each round, with their task being to find and kill all of the survivors before the round timer runs out. The survivors must run or hide to avoid being slain.

Cops and Runners / Hot Pursuit

Based on Halo Reach Hot Pursuit gamemodes, this is a map wherein infected police officers patrol the streets for whatever they consider to be wrongdoing by the surviving drivers. As drivers are killed they become police, and the round ends when everyone is infected. Fun for roleplaying with friends.

This map is essentially just for fun in a spastic and confusing, but oftentimes entertaining world. Good for 3-AM mindless gameplay.
