Polo Demo

This is the game mechanics demo of our 2017-18 TSA Video Game, about Polo.


Description

Be a horse-riding club-swinging ball-chasing polo player in our solution to this year's TSA Video Game Design challenge. A gamepad is required for this version of the game, and obviously - it's a mechanics demo. There are only half the players there are supposed to be, their AIs are garbage, and there's no menu or anything. Just run around, bash the ball, score goals, and watch the AIs glitch out.


Instructions

  • WAD & Left Stick : Move Player
  • Right Stick : Shoot
