Battle Polo Demo

This is the game mechanics demo of our 2017-18 TSA Video Game, about Polo.


Description

Be a horse-riding club-swinging ball-chasing polo player in our solution to this year's TSA Video Game Design challenge. Join in the glitchy multiplayer fun of batting a ball towards a goal, now with your friends. Choose a name, create or join a game, and score meaningless points!


Instructions

  • WAD : Move Player
  • Left Mouse Button : Shoot
