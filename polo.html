Description

Fight in an arena polo-style to score goals against online opponents in our solution to this year's TSA Video Game Design challenge. Join in the multiplayer fun of batting a ball towards a goal, now with your friends. Choose a name, one of seven characters, create or join a game, and score points to earn coins! Then, unlock new characters in the Shop using those coins to dominate the competition! Play through the thrilling story mode, complete with three levels. Or, challenge yourself in challenge mode to earn even more prizes!


Characters

Polo Player
The basic polo player rides a horse and swings a club, and has average stats.
Their club attack has a short range and moderate strength.
Xtra Terrestrial
The spaceship character is fast, but is slow to turn and accelerate.
When holding down LMB, they activate a tractor beam which slowly pulls the ball towards them.
PoloPro3000
The robot character has average stats.
Their attack shoots a lazer projectile, that strongly deflects the ball when hitting it.
General Hartmann
The tank character has a slow speed and turning rate, but powerful fire.
Their attack shoots a large slow bullet, that very strongly deflects the ball when hitting it.
Oof
Oof is a dinosaur-riding caveman with a club and average stats.
Oof's club-swing is more effective than the generic Polo Player at hitting the ball away.

Polo Man
Polo Man is a extremely agile polo-playing superhero.
Polo Man can punch the ball with incredible strength.


Instructions

  • WASD or Arrow Keys : Move Player
  • Left Mouse Button : Attack
  • Escape : Quit Lobby or Game
