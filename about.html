Skills

I am a software developer specializing in front-end development and artificial intelligence. My primary skills include building applications with the LAMP framework, Node.js-Socket.io-Express, and Java with an SQL or MS Access database. I also have skills in web design, using Bootstrap, Wordpress, Joomla, and Drupal. As a hobby, I do game design, mostly through TSA with my teams, using Unity and GameMaker. I would consider myself fluent in Java, PHP, Javascript, HTML, CSS, and Perl, with developing skills in GoLang, Rust, Python, C#, C++, CoffeeScript, Ruby, and Rails. I am certified with MS Word, and also have experience with Blender, Photoshop, Sony Vegas, Adobe Premiere, Movie Studio, MuseScore, and 3D printing using IIIp and Slic3r. I have experience working with a development team using Atlassian Jira, Stride, and Confluence, Slack, Github, and Trello. My experience also includes use of hosting services and development of cloud-based applications, including Amazon Web Services (primarily Lightsail and S3), Heroku, and x10hosting.
